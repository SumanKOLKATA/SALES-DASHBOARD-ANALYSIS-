YTD  - Year till Date - (1st Jan 2023 till 3rd Sept,2023)
IF YEAR([Order Date]) = YEAR([Max_date])
AND 
[Order Date] <= [Max_date]
THEN [Sales]
END

QTD - Quarter till Date (1st July to 31st Sept)
IF DATEDIFF('quarter',[Order Date],[Max_date])=0
AND 
[Order Date] <= [Max_date]
THEN [Sales]
END

MTD - Month till Date - (1st Sept to 3rd Sept)
IF DATEDIFF('month',[Order Date],DATE("2015-03-25"))=0
AND 
[Order Date] <=DATE("2015-03-25")
THEN [Sales]
END

CURRENT YAER SALES CALACULATION 
IF YEAR([Order Date]) = {MAX(YEAR([Order Date]))} THEN [Sales] END

PREVIOUS YAER SALES CALACULATION 
IF YEAR([Order Date]) = {MAX(YEAR([Order Date]))} -1 THEN [Sales] END

YOY SALES GROWTH OR DEFICIT 
(SUM([CURRENT YEAR SALES])-SUM([PREVIOUS YEAR SALES (copy)]))/SUM([PREVIOUS YEAR SALES (copy)])

YOY SALES GROWTH OR DEFICIT IDICATORS ▲▼

FOR SALES SPARKLIN USE MAX AND MIN FORMULA THEN DUAL AXIES

IDENTIFICATION OF MAX AND MIN SALES OF CURRENT YEAR = IF SUM([CURRENT YEAR SALES])= WINDOW_MAX(SUM([CURRENT YEAR SALES])) THEN SUM([CURRENT YEAR SALES]) ELSEIF 
SUM([CURRENT YEAR SALES])= WINDOW_MIN(SUM([CURRENT YEAR SALES])) THEN SUM([CURRENT YEAR SALES]) 
ELSE NULL
END
FOR PROFIT
IF SUM([CY PROFIT])= WINDOW_MAX(SUM([CY PROFIT])) THEN SUM([CY PROFIT]) ELSEIF 
SUM([CY PROFIT])= WINDOW_MIN(SUM([CY PROFIT])) THEN SUM([CY PROFIT]) 
ELSE NULL
END

STATE WISE AVERAGE  CURRENT YEAR SALE
